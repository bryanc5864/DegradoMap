We thank the reviewer. All revisions are in blue in the revised paper.

C1 (Small dataset): Added "Dataset characteristics and limitations" paragraph before any results (L412-416): 155 unique proteins, CRBN 62%/VHL 35%/other 3%, no external validation. These constraints bound generalization. PROTAC-8K is the only public benchmark of this scale. Limitations 1,2,6 at L732-733,737.

C2 (No structural validation): Acknowledged as Limitation 5 (L736). Ternary-complex modeling requires PROTAC linker geometry unavailable in our pre-synthesis setting. Post-hoc validation against experimental ternary complexes is future work. BRD4 case study (L655-672) provides partial structural validation via lysine attention weight analysis.

C3 (No biological case study): Added Section 4.5 "Case Study: BRD4 Degradability" (L655-672). DegradoMap predicts BRD4 (O60885) highly degradable with CRBN (score: 0.87, L659). Lysine attention identifies K374 (attention: 0.12, SASA: 94A^2) as top residue—disordered, solvent-exposed linker between bromodomains (L663). K311 (0.09) and K434 (0.08) are secondary hits (L664-665). Mass spectrometry confirms K374 as a major ubiquitination site (Zengerle et al. 2015, L670). Single case; systematic validation is future work (L672).
